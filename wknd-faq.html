Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about WKND adventures

How do I book an adventure?

Booking an adventure is easy! Simply browse our adventures, select the one you like, choose your preferred date, and complete the booking form. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before your adventure starts. Cancellations made within 48 hours are subject to a 50% fee. No-shows are non-refundable.

What should I bring on an adventure?

Each adventure has specific requirements, but generally you should bring comfortable clothing, water, sunscreen, and a camera. Detailed packing lists are provided in your confirmation email.

Are adventures suitable for beginners?

Yes! We offer adventures for all skill levels. Each adventure listing includes a difficulty rating and requirements. Our guides are experienced in working with beginners.

Do you provide equipment?

Most adventures include all necessary equipment in the price. Specific equipment details are listed on each adventure page. Personal items like clothing and water bottles are not provided.

What if the weather is bad?

Safety is our priority. If weather conditions are unsafe, we'll reschedule your adventure or provide a full refund. We monitor weather closely and will contact you if changes are needed.

Can I bring children?

Many of our adventures are family-friendly! Age restrictions vary by adventure and are clearly marked. Children under 18 must be accompanied by an adult.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team at support@wknd.com or call us at 1-800-WKND-ADV. We're available 24/7 to assist you.
